About Desa Langgahan

Desa Langgahan is a traditional village located in the eastern part of Bali, Indonesia. Nestled among lush rice terraces and surrounded by stunning natural landscapes, this picturesque village offers visitors an authentic glimpse into Balinese rural life and culture.

The village is known for its preserved traditional architecture, ancient temples, and the warmth of its local community. Unlike the more tourist-heavy areas of Bali, Desa Langgahan maintains its authentic charm and provides a peaceful retreat for those seeking to experience the real Bali.

Residents of Desa Langgahan continue to practice traditional farming methods, crafts, and religious ceremonies that have been passed down through generations, making it a living museum of Balinese heritage and traditions.
